Key Lime Pie Recipe

Originally from allrecipes.com. Visit the website and see the recipe here

Hi! My name is Olivia and today I am sharing one of my favorite recipes. Key lime pie is a great summer dessert, perfect for any picnic or dinner party! As stated on the allrecipes.com website, this recipe won first place for the Quick and Easy category in the 1999 American Pie Council's National Pie Championship. So, even if you are new to baking, you can still follow this fun and simple recipe for a delicious treat!

First, let's start with some things you'll need:

  • 5 beaten egg yolks
  • 1 14 oz can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of lime juice
  • 1 9 inch prepared graham cracker crust
  • lime slices (optional)
  • whipped cream (optional)
Prep time Cook time Total time
20 minutes 15 minutes 35 minutes

Once you have gathered all of your ingredients, follow these instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  2. Combine sweetened condensed milk, key lime juice, and egg yolks in a large bowl; mix well.
  3. Pour mixture into unbaked graham cracker crust.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is set, about 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with lime slices and whipped cream if you would like to.
  6. Allow to cool completely before slicing.
